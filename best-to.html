Best to Grab and Go

Coffee Zone

With the largest selection of homemade goods available right at the counter, Coffee Zone is the best place to grab a fresh and delicious snack to go with your coffee without having to wait.

Best to Meet with a Group

Shortwave Coffee

With its spacious interior and large tables, you won't have to worry about not having enough space for you and your group.

Best to Study

Lakota Coffee

A warm, inviting interior, classy decor, and the relaxing sound of coffee roasting right inside the store make Lakota Coffee a great place to relax and focus on your work.

Best to Sit Outside

Acola Coffee

An upstairs section provides Acola Coffee with a large patio connected to its upstairs space. Offering plenty of sunshine and a view of Downtown Columbia, there's no better place to sip on an iced coffee while enjoying the beautiful weather.
