The Best Spots for...

Best to Grab and Go. This section is meant to give coffee lovers a look into the best local shops for specific offerings. Here we will break down where to find the best homemade goods, study space, place to mingle with friends and finally where to enjoy the sunshine.

Coffee Zone

With the largest selection of homemade goods available right at the counter, Coffee Zone is the best place to grab a fresh and delicious snack to go with your coffee without having to wait.

Best to Meet with a Group

Shortwave Coffee

With its spacious interior and large tables, you won't have to worry about not having enough space for you and your group.

Best to Study

Lakota Coffee

A warm, inviting interior, classy decor, and the relaxing sound of coffee roasting right inside the store make Lakota Coffee a great place to relax and focus on your work.

Best to Sit Outside

Acola Coffee

An upstairs section provides Acola Coffee with a large patio. Offering plenty of sunshine and a view of Downtown Columbia, there's no better place to sip on an iced coffee while enjoying the beautiful weather.
