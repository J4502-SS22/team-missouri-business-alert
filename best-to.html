The Best of the Best

This section gives coffee lovers a look into the best local shops for specific offerings and occassions. Here we will break down where to find the best homemade goods, study for finals, mingle with friends and where you can enjoy the sunshine. Think of it like a superlative for a coffee shoppe— and find whatever you're looking for below!




Best to... Get a Coffee Accompaniment

Coffee Zone

With the largest selection of homemade goods available in the large glass case at the counter, Coffee Zone is the best place to grab a fresh and authentic pastry to go with your coffee without having to wait. They have a variety of Middle Eastern treats including baklava, rugelach and other breakfast goodies.

They are also known for their full menu of Middle Eastern delicacies to fulfill any of your breakfast, lunch and dinner needs ranging from gyros to hummus plates.



Best to... Meet with a Group

Shortwave Coffee

With its spacious interior and large family-style table in the center of the room, Shortwave's 9th Street location is the perfect place for you and your whole group to meet. There's even a more private space in the back away from other patrons where all group members can sit and engage with one another without feeling like a disruption to others. And you won't have to worry about this place running out of coffee or treats for your crew to snack on during their time there!



Best to... Study

Lakota Coffee

A warm, inviting interior and the relaxing sound of coffee roasting right in the back of the store makes Lakota Coffee a great place to focus on your work. There are several tables with plug-ins close by to charge up your devices during those long study sessions, and the location's late-night hours make it the perfect place to get your grind on during finals week or on the weekends.

Worried you might have to venture out and lose your spot to get some food during your work session? Have no fear! Lakota has a variety of pastry and snack options, and a menu of sandwiches made-to-order to keep you fueled up all day long.



Best to... Sit Outside

Acola Coffee

The upper-level of Acola Coffee features a walk-out leading to a large patio with various picnic smaller-sized tables. The area brings customers the opportunity to soak up some Vitamin D while getting their fill of delicious, ethically-grown coffee. Offering plenty of sunshine and a different view of Downtown Columbia, there's no better place to sip on an iced coffee while enjoying the beautiful weather during whatever seasonal weather mid-MO decides to bring. And if all else fails, the Acola interior maintains much of the natural-feel of the outside with many of the same views seen from its large glass windows and plants throughout the space.



Best to... Enjoy the Local Arts

Fretboard Coffee

Located in the heart of the North Village Arts District, and connected to multiple art studios, Fretboard is an amazing place to drink coffee and enjoy the arts. The venue also sometimes hosts live music, which takes the atmosphere of Fretboard to new levels.
