Featured and Favorites

This page shows the menus and some of the favorite menu items from each location: Coffee Zone, Fretboard, Lakota, Shortwave, and Acola



Coffee Zone Menu

Simple yet refreshing, Coffee Zone offers some of the best versions of coffee drinks on ice, including our favorite, the iced blonde roast
Fretboard Menu

Their Chai tea Latte is a bold classic. This may be one of the only downtown coffee shops that prides itself on strong coffee rather than sweet flavoring.
Lakota Coffee Menu

Best when mixed with a splash or two of your favorite flavoring, an iced latte from Lakota Coffee is sure to keep you cool on a hot day.
Shortwave Menu

A delicious dessert in a cup, the white chocolate mocha is a perfect selection for anyone with a sweet tooth.
