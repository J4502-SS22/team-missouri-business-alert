Featured and Favorites

This page shows the menus and some of the favorite menu items from each location: Coffee Zone, Fretboard, Lakota, Shortwave, and Acola


Coffee Zone Menu and Featured Drinks



Iced Blonde Roast & Rocket Fuel

Simple yet refreshing, Coffee Zone offers some of the best versions of coffee drinks on ice, including our favorite, the iced blonde roast. The shop is also known for their locally famous pound of Rocket Fuel. This flavored drink has been a hit since the 90's


Fretboard Menu and Featured Drink

Chai Tea Latte

The Chai tea Latte is a bold Fretboard classic. This may be one of the only downtown coffee shops that prides itself on strong coffee rather than sweet flavoring.


Shortwave Menu and Featured Drink

White Chocolate Mocha


A delicious dessert in a cup, the white chocolate mocha is a perfect selection for anyone with a sweet tooth.

Acola Featured Drinks

Sweet Cinnamon Latte & Vanilla Latte

A Sweet Cinnamon Latte from Acola cannot be beat! The Vanilla Latte is a close and delicious second. Acola brews an amazing latte, regardless of your choice of flavoring!
