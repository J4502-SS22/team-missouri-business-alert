Shop-By-Shop Basics

Here we will introduce you to all five independent coffee shops in the downtown area, most of which are located on and/or just off of the main stretch, 9th Street. These five locations, well six technically, are: Acola Coffee Company (formerly Three Story Coffee), Coffee Zone, Fretboard Coffee, Lakota Coffee Company and Shortwave Coffee (boasting two downtown locations).

Get to know the shops, and then click each of their links to learn more before moving on to our other pages!



Acola Coffee Company

(Formerly Three Story Coffee)

Acola Coffee Company, formerly known as Three Story Coffee, was inspired by three basic stories when you drink a cup of coffee: “Yours, ours, and the farmers.” Acola’s main goal is to bridge the gap between coffee growers and coffee drinkers.
They roast their coffee in batches of 18 pounds at a time south of town in Jefferson City, and then serve it up freshly brewed in a two-story building in the North Village Arts District. Here you will find a spacious dining area, more room to lounge up the stairs and a patio for those who like to take in the fresh air. So whatever seating arrangement is your poison, Acola has a place, and a drink for you to enjoy.




Hours: 7 a.m. to 7 p.m. (M-Sat), 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. (Sun)
Address: 300 N. 10th St., Suite 100, Columbia, Mo. 65201
Contact: (573) 447-8050
Acola Coffee Company Website


Coffee Zone

Established in 1994, Coffee Zone is a spacious, warm and inviting location offering not only a variety of specialty espresso drinks, but also an extensive menu of light Mediterranean eats and pastries. So, whether you're looking to get your caffeine-fix for the day or craving an authentic taste of the Middle East, this place has got it all, including dessert, covered! One of the most unique offerings that tends to bring people back again and again is their Turkish coffee, made-to-order and served in its very own cezve, or small, long-handled pot specifically designed for the beverage. Coffee Zone is also home to 'Rocket Fuel,' a rich dark roast with an extra caffeine punch.



Hours: 6:30 a.m. to 9 p.m. (M-Sat), 8 a.m. to 9 p.m. (Sun)
Address: 11 N. 9th St., Columbia, Mo. 65201
Contact: (573) 449-8215
Coffee Zone Website


Fretboard Coffee

Fretboard Coffee’s home base speaks volumes about the level of artistry in the shoppe. This shop was a dream created by a husband and wife duo from the East Coast who wanted to commit themselves to a fresh roastery, sustainability and community impact. Fretboard welcomes the community with unique flavors, open mic nights, and a coffee truck named “Marilyn.” It sources its coffee from ethical and organic growers, and that’s exactly the vibe a customer feels when they sit inside for a cup of coffee. Fretboard has one of the more limited, but also impressive menus on our list, boasting a variety of ways they brew up their beans for the Columbia community.



Hours: 7:30 a.m. to 6 p.m. (M-F), 8 a.m. to 6 p.m. (Sat), 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. (Sun)
Address 1013 E. Walnut St., Columbia, Mo. 65201
Contact: (573) 227-2233
Fretboard Coffee Website


Lakota Coffee Company

Lakota Coffee Company is everything your average coffee drinker could want and need, and even more. The interior features natural brick walls with local artistry hanging on the walls, and if you time it just right, the aroma of freshly roasted coffee beans coming from the back of the store. With a classic downtown building feel, the shop is long and narrow, but has a fair amount of seating for those wanting to stay in with their drink and a line flow to help you get in and out when you're on the go. The menu features everything from hot to iced to blended, and there is even plenty to choose from for non-coffee drinkers as well, such as smoothies, hot cocoa and apple cider. And this location is not shy on accompaniments, boasting a menu of sandwiches and a case of pastries to pair with your beverage. The store also has a wall of coffee bean varieties to pick from and take home after your visit.



Hours: 6 a.m. to 12 a.m. (M-Sat), 6:30 a.m. to 12 a.m. (Sun)
Address: 24 S. 9th St., Columbia, Mo. 65201
Contact: (573) 874-2852
(Note: The above hours, address and contact information apply to the downtown Lakota's location only)
Lakota Coffee Company Website


Shortwave Coffee

Offering a unique variety of espresso drinks, brewed coffee and teas, Shortwave Coffee has a mission to bring some of the best tasting coffee to your tastebuds. Each of their baristas goes through an intense training proceess to learn the trade and ensure thaat Columbia gets the very best they have to offer, ranging from deliciously simple lattes to speciaally crafted hot chocolates for those straying away from caffeine. One of the coolest things to note is that Shortwave has not one, but two locations downtown as of early 2021. Their roastery and primary coffee shop remains in its rare basement-like location of Alley A, while their second location acoss from Lakota's on 9th St. focuses on bringing handmade baked goods to pair with their coffees. Either way, you won't go wrong hitting up this spot for your daily fix.



(Note: Information below is listed in the following order: Alley A Location & 9th St. Location)
Hours: 8 a.m. to 8 p.m. (daily) & 8:30 a.m. to 6 p.m. (daily)
Address: 915 Alley A, Columbia, Mo. 65201 & 29 S. 9th St., Columbia, Mo. 65201
Contact: (573) 214-0880
Shortwave Coffee Website
