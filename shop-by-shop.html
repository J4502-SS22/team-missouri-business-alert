Shop-By-Shop Basics

Here we will introduce you to all five independent coffee shops in the downtown area, most of which are located on and/or just off of the main stretch, 9th Street. These five locations, well six technically, are: Acola Coffee Company (formerly Three Story Coffee), Coffee Zone, Fretboard Coffee, Lakota Coffee Company and Shortwave Coffee (boasting two downtown locations).

Get to know the shops, and then click each of their links to learn more before moving on to our other pages!

Coffee Zone

Coffee Zone is a spacious, warm and inviting location offering not only a variety of specialty espresso drinks, but also an extensive menu of light Mediterranean eats and pastries. So, whether you're looking to get your caffeine-fix for the day or craving an authentic taste of the Middle East, this place has got it all, including dessert, covered!

Hours: 6:30 a.m. to 9 p.m. (M-Sat), 8 a.m. to 9 p.m. (Sun)
Address: 11 N. 9th St., Columbia, Mo. 65201
Contact: (573) 449-8215
